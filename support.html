RoPairly Support

Need help with RoPairly? Use the steps below to troubleshoot common issues before reaching out.

Common Troubleshooting Steps

  • Refresh Roblox and reopen the RoPairly popup.
  • Make sure you are signed in to the Roblox account you want to use with RoPairly.
  • For bio verification, copy the exact RoPairly code into your Roblox About section, save it, then click Verify.
  • If RoPairly shows an account mismatch, only recover the account if the detected Roblox account is yours.
  • If shared games do not appear, refresh Roblox after both users are linked.

What To Include In A Support Request

  • A short description of what happened.
  • Your browser and operating system.
  • Any visible RoPairly error message.
  • The step you were on, such as verification, linking, saving games, or account recovery.

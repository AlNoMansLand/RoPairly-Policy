Common Steps

  • Refresh Roblox and reopen the RoPairly popup.
  • Make sure you are signed in to the correct Roblox account.
  • For bio verification, confirm the exact code is saved in your Roblox About section before clicking Verify.
  • If account recovery is shown, only continue if the detected Roblox account is yours.
